Rabbit Breeds

  • Flemish Giant

    • This is a giant breed.Its body is semi-arch.Weight ranges between
    13-14 pounds. Its fur is classified as roll-back. The breed started in Belgium.

  • Netherland Dwarf

    • This rabbit is a small breed. It has a compact body type. It weighs
    about two pounds. Its fur is roll back. Its breed was recognized in 1969.

  • New Zealand

    • This rabbit is a large breed. Its body type is commercial. It's noted for its meat. The New Zealand
    comes is three colors;red,white, and black. Weight ranges from nine to twelve pounds. Fur is fly back.

  • Checkered Giant

    • This breed is classified as giant. It has a full arch body type.The fur is fly back. It weighs on
    average twelve pounds. This breed first appeared in Germany in 1904.

  • Holland Lop

    • This is a small breed. Its body type is compact. It weighs from three ti four pounds. Fur is roll back.
    Its ears do not stand up. Breed was recognized in 1979.

  • Himalayan

    • This is another small breed but this one has a cylindrical body. Its fur is fly back. It weighs four pounds.
